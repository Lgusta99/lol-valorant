Alistair

Lore Abilities Theme Music

Alistair is a mighty minotaur with a fearsome reputation, born in the heart of the Great Barrier. He seeks vengeance against the Noxian empire for the destruction of his clan. Though he was enslaved and forced into the gladiatorial arenas, Alistair's indomitable will allowed him to survive and eventually escape. Now, he roams the land, a symbol of resistance, ready to crush any who stand in his way.
